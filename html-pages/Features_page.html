“We created Links because we wished we had a platform like this to guide us when we first started university.

Be able to meet people like us, from our community, discover new ones, and not feel alone.

FOR WHAT

We allow university students, whether new or returning, to discover new people and make new connections based on things in common, shared interests, and even shared classes.

FOR WHOM

Our targeted public is mostly the 1st year students.
But it's designed for all UOttawa students.

WHAT DO WE PROPOSE

You will get to:

  • Look up people and get connected to them
  • Chat with your friends
  • Discover communities based on your origin, music taste, favorite activities..., and get in the group discussion
  • Get to know about the upcoming events
  • Get the premium version to have access to more opportunities!
